Buying from Dealerships Vs. Private Sellers

We recommend that you broaden your choices by looking at offers from both dealerships and private sellers. Nonetheless, there are pros and cons to both options.

Dealership Private Seller
Pros
  • more options for vehicles in one dealership
  • dealerships generally have more credibility
  • free vehicle history report
  • may accept trade-in of vehicles
  • can choose from new cars
  • new vehicles, and sometimes even used vehicles, come with warranty
  • more selctions for a specific brand in that brand's dealership
  • can negotiate to lower price
  • usually cheaper
  • wider selection if you're willing to look online
  • can negotiate to lower price
Cons
  • more expensive because dealerships need to cover more costs
  • more travelling required to meet multiple private sellers
  • more risk of buying "lemons" - cars that are actually worse than advertised
